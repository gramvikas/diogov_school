Infrastructure

The institution is functioning in a well ventilated, fully furnished building having the required infrastructure facilities such as spacious lecture halls, science lab, principal room, staff room, spacious lobby etc.

Multipurpose Hall

The institution has a well furnished big assembly hall in the campus which accommodates around 100 students at a time. It also has well ventilated multipurpose hall which accommodates more than 600 people at a time.

Playground

In order to develop all round personality of students, the institution gives equal importance to sports and cultural activities. The management is keen to see that the students grow physically, mentally, intellectually and morally. It has a large playground where games like cricket, football, volleyball, basketball and other games can be played. It also has facilities for indoor games like badminton, carom, chess etc.

Computer Lab

The institution has a computer lab with 14 computers with DVD writer and an over head projector which is being used as a Modern Educational Teaching Aids.
